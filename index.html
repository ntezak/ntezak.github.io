Nikolas Tezak

I am a researcher and engineer interested in novel paradigms for information processing.

Originally born and raised in Leverkusen, Germany, I went to university in Heidelberg and afterwards moved to California where I obtained a PhD in Applied Physics from Stanford University.

I currently live in Oakland with my wife, kid and two cats.

Professional Interests

My PhD work at Stanford under Hideo Mabuchi covered modeling techniques and applications for quantum feedback networks.

At Rigetti Computing I worked on the full stack (hardware and software) for developing, operating and programming quantum computers.

Since joining OpenAI, I have picked up Deep (Reinforcement) Learning for the purpose of developing safe, general purpose artificial intelligence.
